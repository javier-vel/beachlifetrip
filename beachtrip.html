Trip to Playalinda Beach 1-2 People $139.99

Description

The trip starts at 8:00 am from any hotel close to Orlando International Airport in a family minivan 7 passengers or SUV. It takes 1:00 hour to arrive at Lot 1 on Canaveral National Seashore Park. From Lot 1 to 13 swimwear is permitted, only at Lot 13 you can see presence of nude sunbathing. This trip make the stop on Lot 12 you can walk to lot 13 top less, nude or whatever you want is up to you. Due to overflow parking on Lot 13 early in the morning around 9:30 am everyday, the mini van will park at lot 12. Please be discrete. Any lascivious act will be reported to park ranger. No showers available. Restroom available. Pets, drones, metal detector not permitted. Cellular signal is weak. Bring your towel, small bag, sunglasses, beach hat, flip flop, sun blocker and best time to have fun. NOTE: Lot 13 is not recommended for child.

  • Picking up and dropping off from/to any MCO hotels.

  • Sunshade.
  • Beach chair.
  • Water and Snacks.
  • Frisbee and Beach racket with ball.

What not included:

  • Towels (you can rent it for $5).
  • Any alcoholic beverage.
  • Wheelchair accessibility.
  • Any child car sit.

When the trip is confirmed:

  • 24 hours before departing.

When the trip is canceled:

  • Due to inclement weather.
